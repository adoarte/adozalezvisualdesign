LUNATTACK

Art Direction + Photo- Graphic- Web design + Video Creation

Lunattack is an electro pop and rock band led by the female voice of Barbara Duchow. Since 2015 I have worked toguether with Barbara Duchow (Hamburg), founder/ Producer, co-composer and vocalist of Lunattack. I created the logo for the project and designed the debut Album Cover with booklet "Pont Neuf" 2017. I also designed the official website and colaborated like visual artist in all music videos for live performances. 2019 I created the music video "Escape" for the Toulouse band "Elephant Memories" featuring Lunattack. Currently we are editing new music videos and designing new live multimedia experiences. I mannage Lunattack´s YouTube Channel, Facebook, Instagram, Soundcloud, Bandsintown and Spotify accounts too.
