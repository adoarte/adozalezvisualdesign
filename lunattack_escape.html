ESCAPE

Music video creation for "Escape" single by "Elephant Memories" band feat. Lunattack

Since April 2019 Lunattack (Barbara Duchow) started a collaboration with the Toulouse based band “Elephant Memories” singing their songs and doing live performances. "Escape" is a single revisited with Lunattack (vocals).  I created this video clip inspired by a spatial aesthetic with representations of planets, moon, galaxies and the human being. I have made a digital collage from Barbara Duchow´s portraits taking them to moving images in GIF format and then into the video. There are also animated sequences with a surreal stage in space and an old television in which Barbara appears singing.
