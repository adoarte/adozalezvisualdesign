Zahnarzt-praxis
von Schuler Alarcon

Design and drawing vectorization for German Dentistry Technical Dictionary in Sign Language

For this project Zahnarztpraxis von Schuler Alarcon in Hamburg asked me to make illustrations for a German Technical Dictionary in sign language for dental students. I have done photographic and video shooting of an interpreter to capture her gestures for each word in the dictionary. I started the vectorization process using photoshop and made different compositions with the necessary expressions for a word. Compositions were made from one to four drawings to express a single word.
