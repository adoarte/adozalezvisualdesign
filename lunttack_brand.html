Lunattack Logo Design / Brand identity

It was necessary to create a logo that represented Lunattack like the band in a simple way but with some visual noise inspired by the Rock genre. The interventions in each letter are based on the movements of wings of a phoenix bird which is referred to in the song "Infinite" of the first album and represents part of the spirit of this project by Barbara Duchow.
