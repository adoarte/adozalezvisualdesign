SWEN KÄHLERT

Web Design + Photography + Video

I created the website for the based in Hamburg, Gemany visual artist Swen Kählert. The page was designed with a free CMS service. I have done the layout and curation of images and texts. The website shows a selection of his currently artworks like paintings, painting-objetcs and installations. Kählert creates with very diverse materials such as concrete, paint, wood, plastic, adhesive tape and even plants. I have edited also video documentations of  his installations in 2 internationals art festivals.
