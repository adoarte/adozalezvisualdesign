GALERIE HOLZHAUER HAMBURG

Video

The Galerie holzhauer hamburg has been showing contemporary art since May 2004. The gallery owner Angela Holzhauer - art historian and cultural journalist sees her gallery as a laboratory. The interest applies to artists who explore the elementary questions of time, space, image, sound and society in terms of material, content and form. She represents political artists such as the multiple biennialist Georges Adéagbo, the Tony Cragg master students Grabriela LaPachecea and Leon Manoloudaki among others. I have created a video presentation for the "Galerie Holzhauer Hamburg" where the gallerist talks about her work and the artists.
