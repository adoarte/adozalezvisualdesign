VISUAL SONGS

Visual Design and Video art creations for Lunattack's live performances

ROLE

Video Artist
2D Animator
Video Editor

For Barbara Duchow it was very important that visuals for her first production "Pont Neuf" were very poetic and experimental. It is a serie of 12 video art creations focused on illustrations of the songs joining the scenographic space for live performances as well.

Much of the visuals are based on organic aesthetics and from the nature. Barbara as a vocalist appears in only three videos. She did not want to represent the physical image of the band just the spirit of each song through the plasticity of the moving images. I have created an imagery based on various natural elements like water, algae, iron, rust, rocks, earth and animals for "Pont Neuf, Phoenix Physics, Infinite - and Killer Instinct" videos . All under narratives with a lot of movement as a symbol of restlessness, fighting and spiritual ideology. 

Animated portrait playing with a religious context -  "Vodou Buffalo" - or with family origins making reflections on the history - "Schuldig" - are also present in videos with a more figurative and documentary aesthetic.
