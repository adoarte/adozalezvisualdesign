DIVA ADOYEN

Art Direction + Photography + Graphic Design

In 2009 I created with the Venezuelan artist Yenimar López the Internet based performance art project "Diva AdoYen". For this work I have done all the visual part in which I have remained active designing different pieces of digital art ranging from photography, electronic music production (sample and voice recording through digital cameras and cell phones), video clip creations and different art performances documentation.
