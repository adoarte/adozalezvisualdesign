CINEPIPOR ART GALLERY

Logo + Web Design + Magazine + Social Media Graphics/Headers

 Cinepipor Art Gallery is a virtual gallery of contemporary art that supports new talents in the field of visual arts. The gallery annually organizes the international exhibition MAE (Showing Emerging Art) in different spaces in the city of Miami, United States and promote the work of artists around the world. I created its logo with a responsive site for this gallery where the artists and sponsors have a direct and professional contact with it. On it its possible fill out virtual forms to participate directly in its open calls, contract sponsorship services from a price table, check out its virtual magazine and see the work of art of its artists from a photo gallery.
