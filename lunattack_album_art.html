PONT NEUF

Art Direction for Lunattack´s Debut Album 

ROLE 

Art Director
Graphic Designer

The art of "Pont Neuf" is inspired by the powerful messages that a woman with ancestors in the second world war gives to the world through her music. Lunattack delivers a powerful electro-rock-pop experience, with a touching insight into a life story that defies pre-assembled patterns and breaks free of an inconvenient lineage. The album cover was based on the feelings of helplessness, anger and disappointment that Barbara Duchow (author) sweeps comfortably through her stories, as the moments of joy and love take on power and depth.

The album's art is complemented by an illustration of the moon as a reference for the artistic name Lunattack. This is an internal cover for the vinyl record.

BOOKLET

(Color, 6 Pages) it contains three different compositions plus a cover with portraits of the band´s vocals and lyrics.
